Opening a plan or an ongoing from the List

What a List row opens — the same content framed to the platform: a centered dialog on desktop, a bottom sheet on phone. Four sheets, each shown both ways below.

Planned buys: a month, or a deadline that implies one. One switch flips the input. Off → pick the target month directly (what a wishlist drag sets). On → pick a deadline date; the month is derived from it. Either way it files to exactly one month — no contradiction to reconcile. Display: no deadline → shown under the month, no date; with deadline → a by Jul 15 chip, sorted ahead of dateless buys, clay when near. Backend: a nullable Deadline — when set, the period is computed from it; when null, the period is chosen directly (ADR-0018 has neither today).

Shaped by what the backend allows. A plan is immutable except EditHeader (name/category) and Cancel (refund / keep-paid) — its priceless contents and explicit {date, amount} payments can't be edited (ADR-0028/0029). Payments come from the month they fall in — no per-plan account picker; only an extra / early payment dips into a savings account, chosen at pay-time. An Ongoing (Live) also allows ChangeRule, so its Edit carries an editable Repeats block.
