Plan — the definitions library

The default view of Plan, alongside Board and Budget. Everything you've set up to happen, grouped and editable: Ongoing items, Payment plans, and this period's Planned purchases. You edit definitions here; you resolve their instances on Home. This is the screen the missing "where does a definition live" gap was asking for.

Model-true rows. A Payment plan shows what's left + its actual next payment (8 of 24 · next −$320 on Aug 3) — never a fake "per month," since payments are explicit {date, amount} rows (ADR-0029); its row action is Manage (progress + Cancel), not a field edit, because a plan is immutable except cancel (ADR-0028). Planned purchases are period-local (ADR-0018) — shown under the period they're filed on, with no day.
