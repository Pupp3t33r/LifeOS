Exchange rates — every conversion, traceable

The view behind “Open rates ▸” on Home, and behind Settings → Money → Exchange rates. For each pair the app shows the applicable rate, its source (Belarusbank card sell preferred, Frankfurter fallback), the date it applies to, and a Stale badge once it ages past two days. No number in the app is a rate the user can’t trace here — the no-false-precision valve (ADR-0015). A currency filter narrows the list to every pair containing a chosen currency (base or quote) — client-side, ships in v1. Belarusbank rates are quoted against the BYN pivot; cross pairs come from Frankfurter, which stores the full base×base matrix directly (no triangulation).
