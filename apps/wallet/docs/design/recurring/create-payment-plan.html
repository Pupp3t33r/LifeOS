wallet · payment plan — one financed purchase

Payment plan = one item list + a money schedule

A plan is one purchase you're financing: its items live once at the root (what you bought), and the payments below are just money (a date + an amount) that draw the whole thing down. Every item is financed by the whole schedule — so anything paid outside it (an upfront extra, a separately-bought add-on) is its own entry, not an item here. The one rule: payments sum to items.

Structure per ADR-0028 (Proposed, amends 0017/0019): items at the root, payments money-only, Σ balance. Confirming a payment records a proportional slice of the items (ADR-0028 §4), so budgets stay exact — trivial here since the package is all one category. Grouping, "what's in the box," and order status come from the existing ADR-0022 (Wishlist items + Packages) via Line.WishlistItemId — not from the plan.
