Adding, editing & removing a want

The CRUD sheets behind the wishlist — the same content framed to the platform: a centered dialog on desktop, a bottom sheet on phone. Add from the header button; Edit or Remove by opening a want.

You edit the desire, not the status. A want's fields are yours: name, estimate (optional — a want may have no known price yet, ADR-0030), category, notes, and the one-time / repeat flag (ADR-0034's Once | Reusable). Its stage — Idle · Planned · Financed · Bought, shown as Wishing · Planned · Paying off · Bought — is derived and read-only; you move it by planning on the Board or buying on Home, never by editing here. Linking a want to a domain object (ADR-0030's ExternalRef) is out of scope for now. Category is on the want (amends ADR-0022/0034): pick from your user categories; the system/domain categories arrive with linking. Removing a committed want does not cancel its planned buy — the line keeps tracking until you clear it on the Board; nothing's been paid, so no money is affected. A Repeat want's schedule shows read-only here as month chips — edit instances on the Board. A Repeat want also carries a default Unit (a generic dimension — Pieces · Mass · Volume · Length), shown on Repeat sheets; the kg/lb/gal/ft symbol is cosmetic, rendered from the metric/imperial preference.
