Onboarding — a single centred column

"Set up your first month" (Money ADR-0013): the first savings account, display currency, and month-start day. One centred, width-capped question column that scrolls on short viewports. The earlier two-column "savings canvas" preview was dropped — every figure it showed was a pre-setup placeholder ("after setup", "not set yet"), misleading at this stage. The app bar carries a live language switcher (Wallet's first localized surface, ADR-0001).
